Pesto Pasta

Pesto Pasta is one of my favorite recipe because aside from it's simple to make, it's also made out of healthy ingredients.

Preparation Time Cooking Time Total Time Servings
5 minutes 10 minutes 15 minutes 8

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Seasonings
  • Pesto
  • Pasta
  • Parmesan Cheese

Method

  1. Make the Pesto Pasta
  2. Boil the pasta with in a salt and a little bit of oil and drain
  3. Saute the onion in oil then add the pesto paste and seasonings
  4. Mix the pasta with the pesto sauce
  5. Put some Parmesan Cheese on top
